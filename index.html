Running as a sport dates back to ancient times ‚Äî it was one of the first athletic events in human history. The earliest recorded running competitions come from the ancient Olympic Games in Greece (around 776 BC), where athletes competed in footraces called stadion. Over time, running evolved into various disciplines and became a key part of modern athletics.

ü•á Main types of running races

Today, the main types of running races include:

  • Sprints (100m, 200m, 400m) - short, high-speed races.
  • Middle-distance (800m, 1500m) - a mix of speed and endurance.
  • Long-distance (5000m, 10,000m, marathon) - focused on endurance.
  • Cross-country - off-road races over natural terrain.
  • Trail running and ultramarathons - long-distance runs through mountains or forests.

Running remains one of the most universal and accessible sports worldwide !
